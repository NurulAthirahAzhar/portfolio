About Me

Graphic / Multimedia / Creative Designer

I am a dedicated military officer serving in the Royal Signal Corps, bringing over a decade of experience in human resource management, advanced military communication technologies, and computer-aided design. With a strong foundation in Computer Science, I’ve led and supported high-stakes operations where technology and human coordination converge. Currently, I’m embarking on an exciting transition into creative media, expanding my skill set in graphic design, photography, and videography, where I aim to fuse technical precision with artistic expression for a dynamic career shift.

I am a former Captain in the Royal Signal Corps of the Malaysia Armed Forces, with a solid foundation in Computer Science majoring in Computer Security. Throughout my 10-year military career, I was entrusted with providing, maintaining, and operating critical communication and technology systems for the Army. My leadership and technical expertise were key to ensuring successful operations in mission-critical environments.

Education and Skills:

  • Bachelor of Computer Science (Computer Security) – National Defence University of Malaysia
  • Creative Media Entrepreneur Certificate – PERHEBAT
  • Adobe Photoshop & Illustrator Certificate – VIXMATECH Computer Training Centre
  • Scored A in Visual Arts (Pend Seni Visual) at MRSM Kuantan in SPM, reflecting my early creative talent

Military Experience:

  • Served from 2015 to 2025 in the Royal Signal Corps
  • Retired as Captain, responsible for the Army’s communication and technology systems
  • Played a key role in ensuring seamless operations through cutting-edge communication infrastructure

Now transitioning into the creative media industry, I am leveraging my technical precision from the military with my passion into graphic design, photography, and videography. I am eager to bring my unique blend of discipline, leadership, and creativity to new and innovative projects.
